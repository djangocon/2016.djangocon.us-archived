Call For Proposals Is Closing Soon

Categories

  • All
  • General Announcements
  • Sponsor Updates

Atom Feed

15th April 2016 / by Anna Ossowski in General Announcements

There are only a few more days left to submit a proposal to DjangoCon US 2016!

We want to hear about your experience with and around Django. However, talks:

  • Don’t need to be about Django specifically
  • Don’t need to be 100% fleshed out
  • Don’t need to be purely technical
  • Don’t need to be advanced

The conference isn’t until July, so if you have a kernel of an idea, please submit it!

You’ve never spoken before? Not a problem! There are speaker mentors that will help you.

Is cost a concern? Not a problem! Apply for financial aid (deadline is April 25th).

There is no reason not to apply! Here are two articles to get you started:

  • Writing a PyCon Proposal by Brian Curtin
  • How to Submit a Kickass Talk to otsconf (or any other conf for that matter) by the OpenTechSchool Conference

As always, feel free to contact us!

Don’t wait and submit your proposals soon! We hope to see you at DjangoCon US in Philly!
