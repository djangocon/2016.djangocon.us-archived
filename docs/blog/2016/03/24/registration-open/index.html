24th March 2016 / by Anna Ossowski in General Announcements

Registration is open! You may book your tickets and reserve your hotel accommodations for DjangoCon US 2016 now!

We’ve worked hard to make tickets as cheap as they’ve never been before this year, and thus have low prices for students and individuals. Financial assistance is also available to those with limited budgets.

Register for Djangocon US 2016 here. Please don’t wait too long! We’re fairly confident that we’ll sell out again this year.

Reserve your hotel accommodation to take advantage of our discounted room rates. This year the daily room rate for single or double occupancy is $199. This rate is available until June 15th, subject to availability.

We look forward to seeing you in Philly this summer!
